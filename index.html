Sobre

I'm an engineer graduated at University of São Paulo (Brazil) in 2015. In the same year, I began my professional carrer in the automotive industry at MAHLE METAL LEVE where I currently work as a Research and Development Engineer in the company Tech Center. I just concluded a Master degree in Engineering, with a thesis on hard coatings for highly loaded applications. Nowadays, I'm beginning my studies in computer programming focusing in Data Science and Artificial Inteligence. I'm currently living between Jundiai-SP (work), São Roque-SP (parents) and São Paulo (fiancee).
